🌍 Global Energy & Sustainability Dashboard
📌 Project Description
The Global Energy & Sustainability Dashboard is an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze global energy access, renewable energy adoption, CO₂ emissions, electricity generation, and economic indicators across multiple countries from 2000–2020. The dashboard enables users to explore sustainability trends, compare countries, and understand the relationship between energy consumption and economic development.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze global electricity access across countries.
Compare renewable and fossil fuel electricity generation.
Monitor CO₂ emissions over time.
Evaluate renewable energy adoption.
Study the relationship between GDP and sustainability indicators.
Identify countries leading in renewable energy usage.

📈 Key Insights
Renewable energy adoption differs significantly across countries.
Electricity access has improved steadily over the study period.
Higher GDP does not always correspond to higher renewable energy usage.
Some countries maintain high GDP with relatively low CO₂ emissions.
Renewable electricity generation is increasing, though fossil fuels remain dominant in many regions.
Geographic analysis highlights regional differences in sustainability and energy access.
